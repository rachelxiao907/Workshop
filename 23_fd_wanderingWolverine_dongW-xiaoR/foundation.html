Navigation Bar

is a quick and easy way to peruse a website! In Foundation, there is a top-bar class which is the dark grey section of the screen. Then, the navigation bar is broken into two sections: top-bar-left and top-bar-right. Within each left and right class you can create menus, dropdowns, whatever your heart desires...

Grids

Grids can display a different amount of elements per row/column based on the size of your window. (resize your window to see for yourself!) Grids make it easier to see a lot of content, and Foundation adjusts for different sized content
